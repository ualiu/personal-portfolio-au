WORK ORDER TRACKER & CRM FOR SMALL REPAIR SHOPS

I will be building a tool to help small businesses and repair shops digitize work-orders so they can provide a modern customer experience to their customers.

2. WORKFLOW

  • Step 1

    Technician logs into the system and is presented with a dashboard listing the items that need to be repaired, along with their current status.

  • Step 2

    When a new customer walks in, their item description and contact info are added to the database.

  • Step 3

    Technician creates a work ticket with the repair description along with any notes.

  • Step 4

    For everystep in the repair process, the technician logs it in the work order (in-progress/finished), along with the description of the work done.

  • Step 5

    With every step in the repair process, the customer is notified via SMS or email, and provided with a link where they can view the work order on their phone or web.
